About me

I'm a Graphic Designer Web Designer UI/UX Designer

Hello, my name is Katerina Ailamaki and I am a graphic and UI/UX designer from Greece.
Since graduating from the University of West Attica, I have gained professional experience working at a creative agency in Kallithea, serving clients in the healthcare and pharmaceutical sectors.
Alongside agency work, I have freelanced for Devocean , contributing to both graphic design and UI/UX projects.
I also briefly collaborated with Frame , creating headers for restaurants listed on the Box platform.
Passionate about combining creativity with user-centered design, I continuously explore new techniques in digital design, coding, and interactive experiences to deliver thoughtful and effective solutions.
