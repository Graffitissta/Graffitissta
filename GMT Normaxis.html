GTM Normaxis Redesign This was a freelance project I completed in September 2025 for Devocean. The client provided the homepage as a starting point and asked me to continue designing the remaining pages, including About Us, Services, and Contact. My goal was to extend the existing visual style while ensuring a smooth and cohesive user experience across the site. I focused on maintaining consistency, improving layout and interactions, and finding creative solutions that respected the established design framework. This project allowed me to practice problem-solving within constraints while refining my UI/UX skills.
